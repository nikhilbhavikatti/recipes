Ingredients:
 - 1 cup milk
 - 4 teaspoons of sugar
 - 3 teaspoons of tea powder
 - 1 cup water

Directions:
 1.Take a Container and add a cup of Water and keep it for heating.

 2. Add 3 teaspoons of tea powder and 4 spoons of sugar to water and keep it
    on low flame.

 3. When the mixture starts boiling add a cup of milk and heat it until it starts
    boiling.
